How to report a problem

  1. Open the support form (button above) or email us.
  2. Include your app version and device model / iOS version (e.g. "Version 1.0.2 — iPhone 14 Pro / iOS 18.6").
  3. Attach a screenshot if possible — it helps us reproduce the issue faster.

Expected response time

We typically respond within 2–3 business days. For urgent security issues, please include "URGENT" in the subject line of your email.

Privacy

We only collect the information you provide to help troubleshoot and respond to support requests. We will not share personal data with third parties except as required by law.

FAQ

What if I can’t sign in?
Try reinstalling the app. If the issue persists, send us the device log and a screenshot via the support form.
My screenshots were rejected by App Store — who do I contact?
Use the support form and include the rejection reason text you received from App Store Connect.
