How to Report a Problem

     

To get the fastest resolution, please include the following information:

     
           
  1. Use the support form or email address above to start your request.
    2.        
  2. Include your **App Version** and **Device Model / Operating System** (e.g. "Version 1.0.2 — iPhone 14 Pro / iOS 18.6"). You can usually find the version number in the app's settings.
    3.        
  3. Describe the issue or suggestion clearly. Attaching a **screenshot** or **video** helps us reproduce the issue faster.
    4.      
   
           
     

Additional Information

     

Expected Response Time

     

We typically respond to all support requests within 2–3 business days. For urgent issues that prevent you from using the app, please include "URGENT" in the subject line of your email.

     

Privacy

     

We only collect the information you provide to help troubleshoot and respond to support requests. We will not share personal data with third parties except as required by law.

     

Frequently Asked Questions

     
       
What if I can’t sign in?
       
Try force-closing and relaunching the app. If the issue persists, please try reinstalling it. If you still can't sign in, use the support form and include the exact error message you are seeing.
